Badge

Badges are small circles, positioned either at top-right or bottom-right of the parent component. Badge can be used to display notifications count, online / offline status, depending on where they are used.

Badges on Avatars

It includes Profile Badge that is used to show the current status of a user. It can be used with 3 variants : online | offline | dnd

Badges on Icons

It includes Number Badge that is used to show the no. of items present in the parent component.
